Training Programs

OUR GROUPS

  • Children's Tennis 4-6 years (red/ball)

    A great opportunity to engage your child in sports. It's not just training, but also a boost of positive emotions.

  • Children 6-8 years (orange/ball) intensive

    At this age, lessons turn into a playful journey. Children learn to play, handle challenges, navigate, and overcome difficulties.

  • Children 8-10 years (Green/ball) intensive

    At this age, the most capable and talented children can be identified for further development.

  • Children 10-12 years intensive

    This is the age of young professionals, and each of them already knows what they want.

  • Children 12-14 years intensive

    The program is designed for experienced players who are testing their skills in TE-ITF tournaments.

  • PRIVATE TRAINING

    This is an opportunity to achieve more for an optimal level of play and to discover tennis from a different perspective.

About Us

We are a professional team of coaches working with children who strive to achieve great heights in sports.
Our goal is to help everyone find and unlock their potential, improve physical fitness, and confidently move toward their goals.
